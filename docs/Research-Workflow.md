# Choose Better Tech Research Workflow

## Purpose

Define the standard research process used before publishing reviews, guides, and comparisons.

## Source Priority

Research should follow this order:

1. Official documentation
2. Independent reviews
3. Security audits or benchmarks when applicable
4. Reddit and community discussions
5. Public user feedback
6. Competitor content

## Evidence Categories

Every major claim should be categorized as one of:

- Official Claim
- Verified Fact
- Independent Evidence
- Community Opinion
- Weak or Unsupported Claim

## CBTEP

CBTEP stands for Choose Better Tech Evidence Packet.

Every major article should be supported by a CBTEP or equivalent research packet.

A CBTEP should include:

- Product snapshot
- Problem statement
- Target audience
- Official claims
- Verified evidence
- Beginner questions
- Recurring complaints
- Competitor matrix
- Decision snapshot
- Evidence gaps
- Editorial warnings

## Research Rule

Do not write the article until the evidence has been collected and reviewed.
