## Google Drive / Google One / Google Workspace

- **Provider or opportunity:** Google Workspace editions containing Drive; not a consumer Google One program
- **Monetization model:** Per-new-user acquisition commission
- **Direct or network:** CJ Affiliate
- **Public availability:** Available in supported countries for approved publishers
- **Application requirements:** CJ publisher plus advertiser approval; Google evaluates traffic, business relevance, content quality, and performance potential
- **Traffic requirements:** Public page positions the affiliate program for publishers referring at least 100 users per year
- **Commission/revenue model:** Country/edition-specific one-time payment per eligible new user; public examples are not consumer Google One commissions
- **Recurring or one-time:** One-time
- **Cookie duration:** Status unverified publicly
- **Geographic restrictions:** Program lists 40+ countries; account and referral country matter
- **Payout terms:** Through CJ; account/campaign terms must be captured before use
- **Promotion/brand-bidding rules:** Status unverified in public summary; login-gated terms required
- **CBT current status:** CBT uses CJ for other advertisers; no Google Workspace record in `src/data/affiliateLinks.ts`
- **Evidence:** https://workspace.google.com/affiliate-program/ ; https://www.cj.com/publisher
- **Commercial fit:** Later business-intent opportunity, not a direct monetization path for this consumer comparison
- **Editorial conflict risk:** High if Workspace commission causes Google One/Drive to receive favorable consumer treatment
- **Compliance risk:** Do not send consumer Google One clicks through an unrelated Workspace offer
- **Recommended action:** Apply after traffic growth, in a separately authorized task
- **Refresh trigger:** Before any application or if Google publishes a consumer Google One program
