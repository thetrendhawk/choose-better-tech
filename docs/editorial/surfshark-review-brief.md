Do not claim Surfshark makes users anonymous.

# Required Nuance

The review should clearly explain:

- Surfshark is best described as a no-activity-logs VPN with limited operational metadata.
- Temporary user ID/IP and connection-to-server timestamp metadata is reported as deleted within 15 minutes after a session ends.
- Account and billing data can be retained longer.
- Unlimited simultaneous connections are a major strength, but device claims remain volatile before publication.
- Streaming evidence is mixed across independent sources and must be verified close to publication.
- Surfshark's app transparency is weaker than open-source rivals.
- Surfshark has repeated audit activity, but audit scope and public report access have limits.
- Dausos is promising but still has maturity and follow-up-review questions in the approved research.
