## Prohibited publication claims

- Defender is the best, perfect, invulnerable, or enough for everyone.
- Paid antivirus is always better, always unnecessary, or automatically improves detection.
- CBT tested installation, speed, detection, false positives, support, cancellation, family controls, or bundle features.
- A current lab result is a permanent product rank.
- Any VPN, password manager, identity-monitoring, backup, or support feature is inherently high quality because it is bundled.
- Consumer guidance applies to managed business endpoints.
