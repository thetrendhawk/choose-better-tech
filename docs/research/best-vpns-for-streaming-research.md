# Best VPNs for Streaming — Research Record

Checked: 2026-07-12

## Intent

This guide answers which VPN best fits lawful streaming on supported devices. It is distinct from travel, family, beginner, free-plan, and general VPN-value guides.

## Framework

- Streaming access is never guaranteed. Platforms change licensing rules, detection, IP reputation, and terms.
- Native TV apps, router support, Smart DNS-style tools, device limits, support, speed, and refund terms matter more than raw server count.
- Smart DNS and MediaStreamer-style tools are not encrypted VPN tunnels.
- No hands-on streaming or speed testing was performed.

## Candidate conclusions

- NordVPN: best overall fit based on broad platform support, SmartPlay, 10-device documentation, and extensive troubleshooting materials.
- Surfshark: best for many-device households because current materials position unlimited simultaneous devices.
- ExpressVPN: best for simple TV/router setup and broad streaming-device documentation; connection limits vary by plan.
- Proton VPN: best privacy-focused option and free backup, but paid-plan streaming fit is separate from the free plan.
- Mullvad: privacy-first non-affiliate alternative, not a streaming-first recommendation.
