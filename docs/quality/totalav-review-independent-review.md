## Verdict challenge

The reviewer tested whether 18/18 AV-TEST and 99.5% AV-Comparatives results justified making TotalAV a leading recommendation. The verdict remains qualified because AV-Comparatives' annual award was Approved rather than Top-Rated, SE Labs measured 97% protection with one missed targeted attack, and official subscription terms create avoidable purchase complexity. The article does not deny strong protection evidence or convert billing complexity into a claim that the product is fake.
