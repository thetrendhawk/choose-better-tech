# Choose Better Tech Editorial Standards

## Purpose

Define the editorial rules for every article published by Choose Better Tech.

## Every Article Must Answer

- What problem does this solve?
- Who is it for?
- Who should avoid it?
- What is the biggest strength?
- What is the biggest weakness?
- What is the recommendation?
- What is the confidence level?

## Required Article Elements

Every review or guide should include:

- Affiliate disclosure
- Executive summary
- Decision snapshot
- Pros and cons
- Alternatives
- Frequently asked questions
- Final verdict
- Last updated date
- Internal links to related content

## Tone

Write for beginners.

Assume the reader is curious, uncertain, and not deeply technical.

Avoid:

- hype
- fear tactics
- unsupported superlatives
- generic AI-sounding language
- keyword stuffing
- fake urgency

## Recommendation Outcomes

Allowed recommendation statuses:

- Recommended
- Recommended with Reservations
- Needs More Research
- Not Recommended

Never force a recommendation.

## Trust Standard

If the article would not still be useful without affiliate links, it is not ready to publish.
