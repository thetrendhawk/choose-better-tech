# Choose Better Tech Opportunity Scoring v1.0

## Purpose

Score article opportunities before investing time in research and publishing.

Maximum score: 100.

## Scoring Criteria

### Buyer Intent — 25 points

Does the reader appear close to making a purchase decision?

### Affiliate Opportunity — 20 points

Can the article realistically generate affiliate commissions?

### Evergreen Value — 15 points

Will the article remain useful for multiple years?

### Search Opportunity — 15 points

Is there a reasonable chance to earn organic traffic over time?

### Internal Link Value — 10 points

Will this article strengthen existing or future content clusters?

### Production Efficiency — 10 points

Can the article be produced efficiently using existing research and templates?

### Brand Authority — 5 points

Does the article strengthen Choose Better Tech's reputation?

## Priority Bands

90–100:
Build immediately.

80–89:
High priority.

70–79:
Useful, but schedule later.

Below 70:
Defer unless strategically necessary.
