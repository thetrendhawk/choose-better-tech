# Choose Better Tech Update, Corrections, and Date Policy

Version: 1.0
Status: Binding
Owner: Choose Better Tech
Effective Date: 2026-07-12

## Substantive Date Changes

Advance `dateModified` or a visible update date only when:

- important facts were rechecked;
- a recommendation changed;
- a material section was added;
- pricing or plan data was refreshed;
- the source record was updated;
- a meaningful correction was made;
- current testing was performed.

Do not advance dates merely for formatting, typo fixes, internal links, schema formatting, minor wording, or superficial freshness.

## Corrections

For material corrections:

- record the original issue;
- record the corrected claim;
- record the date;
- update research evidence;
- update the claim ledger;
- update `operations/CHANGELOG.md`;
- determine whether a visible correction note is needed.

## Significant Corrections Log

The public site should disclose a correction when reader understanding, safety, trust, or the material decision could have been affected. Examples include:

- a verdict changed;
- a major provider fact was wrong;
- an affiliate relationship was misstated;
- security or privacy guidance was materially incorrect;
- fabricated or unsupported testing was removed.

Minor typographic or formatting fixes do not need a public correction notice. Significant correction history must not be silently erased.
