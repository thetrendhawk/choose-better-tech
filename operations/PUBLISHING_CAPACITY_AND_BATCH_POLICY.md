# Choose Better Tech Publishing Capacity and Batch Policy

Version: 1.0
Status: Binding
Owner: Choose Better Tech
Effective Date: 2026-07-12

## Purpose

Ensure publication speed never exceeds research, review, and maintenance capacity. These are internal capacity controls, not an attempt to manipulate search engines.

## Rules

- Default to one major ranking or comparison per workflow.
- Do not autonomously publish multiple major articles in one run.
- Do not begin a new major article until the previous one reaches its correct final state.
- Do not publish more than two substantial new pages in one calendar day without explicit authorization.
- Remediation work does not justify adding unrelated new pages.
- Batch research may be allowed, but drafting, approval, deployment, and final status remain page-specific.
- A five-page audit may be combined, but each page must be repaired separately.

## Automatic Publication Pause

Pause publication when:

- the evidence backlog grows;
- volatile claims expire;
- unresolved QA failures exist;
- indexing anomalies appear;
- major factual corrections are pending;
- operations documentation is stale.

Resume only after the triggering condition is documented and resolved under the applicable binding policy.
