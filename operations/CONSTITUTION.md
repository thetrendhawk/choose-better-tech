# Preamble

Choose Better Tech exists to help people make informed technology decisions through trustworthy, evidence-based publishing.

We believe technology should improve people's lives, not overwhelm them.

Our responsibility is to reduce confusion through careful research, transparent reasoning, and clear communication.

This Constitution establishes the principles that govern every decision made by Choose Better Tech.

Processes may evolve.

Technology will evolve.

The principles contained within this Constitution should change only with deliberate consideration.
