## Editorial QA

- [ ] Article answers the reader's main decision question.
- [ ] Recommendation is contextual.
- [ ] Pros and cons are clear.
- [ ] Alternatives are included.
- [ ] Limitations are explained.
- [ ] No unsupported superlatives are used.

## Evidence QA

- [ ] Important claims are sourced.
- [ ] Pricing is current.
- [ ] Product details are current.
- [ ] Evidence gaps are disclosed or resolved.

## Affiliate QA

- [ ] Affiliate disclosure is visible.
- [ ] Affiliate links support reader value.
- [ ] Link attributes are appropriate.
- [ ] Partner requirements are followed.

## SEO QA

- [ ] Meta title is clear.
- [ ] Meta description is clear.
- [ ] Headings are semantic.
- [ ] Internal links are present.
- [ ] Related articles are included where useful.

## Technical QA

- [ ] Page renders correctly.
- [ ] Mobile layout is usable.
- [ ] Tables are readable.
- [ ] Build passes when application files change.
- [ ] Sitemap or indexing workflow is updated when needed.
