# Choose Better Tech Topic Cluster Approval Policy

Version: 1.1
Status: Binding
Owner: Choose Better Tech
Effective Date: 2026-07-12

## Approval Gate

Before a new category or content pillar begins, document:

- audience fit;
- mission fit;
- relationship to existing CBT topics;
- evidence access;
- expertise plan;
- minimum viable content cluster;
- internal-link structure;
- monetization independence;
- maintenance capacity;
- volatility burden;
- competitive differentiation;
- original contribution.

Store approval at:

`docs/strategy/<cluster>-cluster-approval.md`

No research or article workflow may begin for an unapproved new cluster. A single opportunistic affiliate article does not justify a new category.

## Parallel Monetization Workstream

Every approved substantial cluster must create and maintain:

`docs/monetization/<cluster>-monetization-opportunity-map.md`

The map and its independent review must satisfy `operations/MONETIZATION_RESEARCH_STANDARD.md`. It must research current and potential provider programs beyond CBT's existing partnerships, assess realistic non-affiliate models, keep public availability separate from CBT status, preserve conflicts, and record `Monetization Independence Check: PASS` or `FAIL`.

Monetization may inform category economics, maintenance capacity, application planning, and later outreach. It may not influence cluster approval, editorial candidates, rankings, verdicts, labels, evidence interpretation, or publication conclusions.

A `FAIL` blocks commercial implementation. Research alone does not authorize applications, outreach, negotiation, tracked links, CTA changes, or editorial changes.

## Cloud Storage

Cloud storage must be treated as a deliberate cluster decision, not an assumed permanent expansion, until reviewed under this policy. Existing cloud-storage pages remain in place and are not changed by this policy installation.

The Cloud Storage monetization map is maintained at `docs/monetization/cloud-storage-monetization-opportunity-map.md`. Proton Drive drafting remains governed by its approved editorial research and may proceed only after the monetization-governance pull request is independently approved and merged.
