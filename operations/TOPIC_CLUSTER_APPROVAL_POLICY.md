# Choose Better Tech Topic Cluster Approval Policy

Version: 1.0
Status: Binding
Owner: Choose Better Tech
Effective Date: 2026-07-12

## Approval Gate

Before a new category or content pillar begins, document:

- audience fit;
- mission fit;
- relationship to existing CBT topics;
- evidence access;
- expertise plan;
- minimum viable content cluster;
- internal-link structure;
- monetization independence;
- maintenance capacity;
- volatility burden;
- competitive differentiation;
- original contribution.

Store approval at:

`docs/strategy/<cluster>-cluster-approval.md`

No research or article workflow may begin for an unapproved new cluster. A single opportunistic affiliate article does not justify a new category.

## Cloud Storage

Cloud storage must be treated as a deliberate cluster decision, not an assumed permanent expansion, until reviewed under this policy. Existing cloud-storage pages remain in place and are not changed by this policy installation.
