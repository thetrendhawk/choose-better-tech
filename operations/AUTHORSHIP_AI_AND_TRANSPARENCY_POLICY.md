# Choose Better Tech Authorship, AI, and Transparency Policy

Version: 1.0
Status: Binding
Owner: Choose Better Tech
Effective Date: 2026-07-12

## Editorial Responsibility

Choose Better Tech remains accountable for every published claim, recommendation, correction, and disclosure regardless of which human or automated system assisted the work.

## Role of Automation

Automation may assist with source collection, research organization, drafting, implementation, validation, deployment checks, and operations documentation. Automation does not remove CBT's responsibility to satisfy the binding evidence, independence, reviewer, and correction standards.

## Accountable Owner

Each substantial page must identify an accountable owner in its internal research and quality records. Public author or reviewer identification must be accurate. Do not imply a named human personally tested, authored, or reviewed something unless that is true.

## Required Transparency

Substantial content must accurately disclose, where applicable:

- testing status;
- visible sources;
- affiliate relationships;
- author and independent reviewer in internal records;
- the last substantive review date;
- a corrections contact method.

Approved general disclosure:

> Researched and reviewed under Choose Better Tech's documented editorial process. Automation may assist with source collection, drafting, validation, and publishing operations. Recommendations remain subject to CBT's evidence, independence, and review standards.

## Future Public Policy Page

Recommend, but do not implement as part of this policy installation, a future page at:

`/editorial-policy-and-corrections`

It should cover editorial independence, research methods, testing policy, AI-assistance policy, affiliate policy, updates, corrections, and a contact method.
