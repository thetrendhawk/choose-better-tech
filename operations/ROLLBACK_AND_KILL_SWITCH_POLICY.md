# Choose Better Tech Rollback and Kill-Switch Policy

Version: 1.0
Status: Binding
Owner: Choose Better Tech
Effective Date: 2026-07-12

## Triggers

Emergency response may be required for:

- fabricated testing;
- an unsupported major verdict;
- plagiarism;
- a serious factual error;
- dangerous security advice;
- a wrong affiliate disclosure;
- a broken canonical;
- accidental `noindex`;
- privacy or legal risk;
- a compromised article;
- a major rendering failure;
- a duplicate-content accident;
- invalid schema causing broad problems.

## Available Actions

- remove the page from navigation;
- remove affiliate CTAs;
- add a correction notice;
- revert the responsible commit;
- temporarily apply `noindex`;
- temporarily unpublish;
- redirect only when justified;
- preserve evidence and history;
- pause automation;
- notify the project owner.

Choose the least destructive action that protects readers and the site while the issue is investigated.

## Rules

- Do not delete research or correction history.
- Do not silently hide serious errors.
- `noindex` is temporary and must be documented.
- Unpublishing requires a restoration or resolution plan.
- Record the trigger, decision, owner, timestamp, affected URLs, action, and resolution.
- Preserve the relevant commit and evidence trail.
- Publication remains paused until the owner or independent reviewer confirms resolution.
