# SOP: Distribution

Version: 1.0  
Status: Active  
Owner: Choose Better Tech  
Last Updated: 2026-07-02

## Purpose

Define the post-publication distribution workflow for each new article.

## Procedure

| Step | Channel | Action | Output |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Website | Confirm live URL and internal links. | Published article ready for promotion. |
| 2 | Pinterest | Create or schedule pins for the article. | Pinterest distribution record. |
| 3 | Instagram | Create supporting post or story concept. | Instagram distribution record. |
| 4 | LinkedIn | Share professional summary when relevant. | LinkedIn distribution record. |
| 5 | Newsletter | Add to future newsletter queue when active. | Newsletter item. |
| 6 | Operations | Update content calendar distribution columns. | Current pipeline status. |

## Pinterest

- Create pins that match the article's reader problem.
- Use clear titles and practical benefit statements.
- Track pin date and click performance.

## Instagram

- Repurpose the article into simple educational takeaways.
- Avoid unsupported claims or urgency.
- Track post date and follower changes.

## LinkedIn

- Use for professional software decision topics.
- Lead with the business or productivity decision.
- Link to the article when appropriate.

## Future Newsletter

- Add strong articles to the newsletter queue.
- Use newsletter content to summarize the reader decision and link back to the full article.
- Track subscriber growth once the channel is active.
