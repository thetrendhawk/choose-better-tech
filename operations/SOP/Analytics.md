Last Updated: 2026-07-02

## Purpose

Define how analytics and webmaster tools are reviewed for business decisions.

## Tools

| Tool | Purpose | Review Cadence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Google Analytics | Traffic, engagement, returning visitors, and conversion context. | Weekly and monthly |
| Google Search Console | Organic clicks, impressions, queries, pages, and indexing. | Weekly |
| Bing Webmaster Tools | Bing indexing, search visibility, and technical search signals. | Weekly |
| Future Dashboards | Consolidated business reporting. | Monthly |

## Weekly Review

- Check top pages by traffic.
- Check indexed and unindexed content.
- Review search queries and impressions.
- Identify distribution opportunities.
- Update `KPI_DASHBOARD.md` and `CONTENT_CALENDAR.md`.

## Monthly Review

- Compare monthly traffic, impressions, and clicks.
- Review affiliate applications, approvals, and revenue.
- Identify top-performing content clusters.
- Decide whether articles need refreshes.
- Set next month's goals.

## Future Dashboard Review

- Consolidate search, analytics, affiliate, and social metrics.
- Track trends across content clusters.
- Use dashboard findings to update roadmap priorities.
