# SOP: Affiliate

Version: 1.0  
Status: Active  
Owner: Choose Better Tech  
Last Updated: 2026-07-02

## Purpose

Define the standard workflow for affiliate program research, application, approval, integration, and maintenance.

## Procedure

| Stage | Action | Output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Research | Identify relevant programs and requirements. | Affiliate opportunity list. |
| Application | Confirm partnership readiness and submit application. | Application log entry. |
| Approval | Record approval terms and program details. | Affiliate record. |
| Integration | Add approved links and disclosures where appropriate. | Updated content. |
| Maintenance | Review terms, links, performance, and compliance. | Current affiliate records. |

## Research

- Identify programs aligned with existing or planned content.
- Review commission model, cookie duration, restrictions, and approval criteria.
- Score the opportunity against audience fit and editorial integrity.

## Application

- Complete the partnership readiness checklist.
- Use current business identity materials once canonical documents are imported.
- Record application date, partner, category, and follow-up date.

## Approval

- Store program terms in an affiliate record.
- Confirm disclosure requirements.
- Confirm allowed promotion channels.
- Confirm link format and tracking requirements.

## Integration

- Add affiliate links only where they support reader decisions.
- Keep recommendations evidence-based.
- Include visible disclosures.
- Verify links after implementation.

## Maintenance

- Review affiliate links monthly.
- Review program terms quarterly.
- Remove or update links for inactive or non-compliant programs.
- Track revenue in the KPI dashboard.
