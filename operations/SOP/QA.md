# SOP: Quality Assurance

Version: 1.0  
Status: Active  
Owner: Choose Better Tech  
Last Updated: 2026-07-02

## Purpose

Provide a standard publication checklist for reviewing articles before and after publication.

## Publication Checklist

| Area | Check | Status |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Editorial | Article answers the primary reader decision question. | TBD |
| Editorial | Recommendation is contextual and not overstated. | TBD |
| Editorial | Pros, cons, alternatives, and limitations are included. | TBD |
| Evidence | Important claims are supported by sources. | TBD |
| Evidence | Pricing and product details are current. | TBD |
| Affiliate | Affiliate disclosure is visible where relevant. | TBD |
| Affiliate | Sponsored or affiliate links use appropriate attributes. | TBD |
| SEO | Title and description are clear. | TBD |
| SEO | Headings are semantic and scannable. | TBD |
| SEO | Internal links are included. | TBD |
| UX | Tables and cards are readable on mobile. | TBD |
| UX | Calls to action appear after useful context. | TBD |
| Technical | Build passes when application files change. | TBD |
| Technical | Route works and page renders correctly. | TBD |
| Operations | Content calendar updated. | TBD |
| Operations | KPI or changelog updates completed if needed. | TBD |

## Approval Rule

An article should not be published until the material issues in the checklist are resolved or explicitly accepted.
