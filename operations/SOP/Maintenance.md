# SOP: Maintenance

Version: 1.0  
Status: Active  
Owner: Choose Better Tech  
Last Updated: 2026-07-02

## Purpose

Define recurring maintenance reviews for content, operations, analytics, affiliate systems, and business documentation.

## Monthly Review

| Area | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Content | Review published articles for outdated pricing, product details, and broken links. |
| Analytics | Update KPI dashboard with available monthly metrics. |
| Affiliate | Check affiliate links, approvals, and revenue. |
| Distribution | Review social posting and click performance. |
| Operations | Update project status and content calendar. |

## Quarterly Review

| Area | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Roadmap | Confirm whether current phase priorities are still correct. |
| Content Clusters | Review performance by category and cluster. |
| Affiliate Programs | Review partner terms, compliance, and revenue potential. |
| SOPs | Update procedures based on actual workflow changes. |
| Legal and Disclosure | Review disclosure, privacy, and terms pages for needed updates. |

## Annual Review

| Area | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Business Strategy | Review mission, audience, revenue model, and category focus. |
| Content Portfolio | Decide which articles to refresh, consolidate, expand, or retire. |
| Analytics | Review annual growth and revenue trends. |
| Partnerships | Review partner mix and long-term fit. |
| Operations | Archive outdated records and refresh core documentation. |
