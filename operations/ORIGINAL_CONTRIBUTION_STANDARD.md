# Choose Better Tech Original Contribution Standard

Version: 1.0
Status: Binding
Owner: Choose Better Tech
Effective Date: 2026-07-12

## Purpose

A substantial Choose Better Tech page must provide meaningful value beyond:

- summarizing vendor pages;
- paraphrasing competing articles;
- repeating common advice;
- combining generic facts;
- targeting a keyword without a distinct reader need.

This standard supplements `operations/EDITORIAL_STANDARDS.md` and the research gate in `operations/RESEARCH_AND_EVIDENCE_STANDARD.md`.

## Original-Contribution Question

Every research record must answer:

> What does this page contribute that readers cannot get as clearly, accurately, or usefully from existing sources?

## Acceptable Original Contributions

Examples include:

- a new evidence matrix;
- current fact verification;
- source-conflict analysis;
- distinctions competitors blur;
- ownership or privacy analysis;
- migration guidance;
- offboarding guidance;
- scenario-based recommendations;
- a user-fit framework;
- first-hand testing;
- primary-source synthesis;
- a practical checklist grounded in evidence;
- meaningful correction of common misconceptions;
- side-by-side plan or feature normalization;
- explicit uncertainty competitors hide.

## Insufficient Contributions

The following do not pass alone:

- “more comprehensive”;
- “easy to understand”;
- “updated for this year”;
- rewritten vendor descriptions;
- a longer FAQ;
- more keywords;
- generic pros and cons;
- prettier formatting;
- an affiliate comparison table.

## Required Research-Record Field

Every substantial-page research record must include:

```text
Original Contribution:
Evidence of Contribution:
Competing Coverage Reviewed:
Information Gap:
Pass/Fail:
```

## Publication Gate

A page with `Original Contribution: FAIL` must not be drafted or published. Its workflow status is:

`RESEARCH INCOMPLETE`

This is a fail-closed gate under `operations/PUBLISHING_WORKFLOW.md`.

## Refreshes

A refresh must add substantive value, not merely update dates or rearrange wording. Date handling must also comply with `operations/UPDATE_CORRECTIONS_AND_DATE_POLICY.md`.
