# Choose Better Tech Sitewide Quality Audit Policy

Version: 1.0
Status: Binding
Owner: Choose Better Tech
Effective Date: 2026-07-12

## Triggers

Run a whole-site audit:

- every 10–15 substantial new pages;
- before opening a major new content pillar;
- after a major algorithmic or traffic anomaly;
- after automation-process changes;
- quarterly when page volume justifies it.

## Audit Scope

Review:

- overlapping intent;
- keyword cannibalization;
- thin pages;
- orphan pages;
- broken links;
- outdated volatile claims;
- inconsistent recommendations;
- missing sources;
- duplicated introductions;
- repeated FAQs;
- excessive template reuse;
- stale affiliate status;
- schema mismatches;
- missing methodology;
- weak internal linking;
- pages that should merge;
- pages that should be removed or redirected;
- content-cluster imbalance.

## Output

Store the audit at:

`docs/audits/sitewide-quality-audit-YYYY-MM-DD.md`

Each page must receive one documented decision:

- `Keep`;
- `Refresh`;
- `Merge`;
- `Redirect`;
- `Remove from promotion`;
- `Temporarily noindex`;
- `Retire`.

Do not remove or redirect a page solely because it has low traffic. Decisions require reader-value, evidence, intent, technical, or maintenance reasoning and must follow `operations/ROLLBACK_AND_KILL_SWITCH_POLICY.md` where applicable.
